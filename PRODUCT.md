# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Researchers, journalists, community members, and careful shoppers exploring a large public grocery catalog on desktop or mobile. They need to find products, inspect price and promotion history, understand missing observations, and move across thousands of records without learning the data format first.

## Product Purpose

Make Gala Fresh Baldwin's anonymously visible online catalog history transparent and practically explorable. Success means every indexed item is reachable, the current result subset is always clear, item evidence is visible without losing list context, and research data remains easy to export and cite.

## Brand Personality

Calm, exact, candid. The interface should feel like a well-made public research instrument: serious without being institutional, dense without becoming cramped, and helpful without behaving like a retail promotion.

## Anti-references

Do not resemble a generic SaaS dashboard, a card-heavy analytics template, or a grocery storefront optimized to push purchases. Do not hide the majority of results behind an unexplained cap, bury selected-item evidence below a long list, or make users guess whether filters are active.

## Design Principles

1. Show the scope: always expose total records, filtered records, current page, and observation date.
2. Keep context: selection and item evidence stay beside the result list on wide screens.
3. Make every record reachable: search, filters, sorting, pagination, direct links, and export all operate on the full index.
4. Prefer evidence over decoration: regular prices, promotions, IDs, categories, and gaps get the visual hierarchy.
5. Explain state plainly: loading, empty, filtered, missing, and baseline-only states say exactly what is happening.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. All controls have visible labels and focus states, color is never the only state signal, tables remain readable with keyboard and assistive technology, motion respects reduced-motion preferences, and the responsive layout preserves complete functionality.
